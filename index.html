About Us

About Chinatown

Let us introduce ourselves

Since 2005, we have been committed to bringing the very best Chinese food to the Halifax area; moreover, with H.K Wong’s Palace in Dartmouth and the old Chinatown in Bedford, we have worked to establish familiar meeting places for the growing Canadian/Chinese community in this great city.

The purchase of this property has allowed us the freedom to deliver on our dream, at a whole new level. Unlike our previous restaurants, this space has been designed (from the ground up) to deliver the most authentic experience possible. We hope you enjoy the food, the view and the company at out first fully realized Chinatown experience.

— Jane Chen
